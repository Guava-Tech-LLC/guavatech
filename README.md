## What We Do

Guava Tech LLC is a boutique AI consulting firm specializing in two high-impact services:

### Document Intelligence
Automated understanding of complex documents — invoices, logistics reports, scientific papers, and forms — powered by multimodal LLMs with production-grade reliability.

- Extract structured data from invoices, contracts, and reports
- Handles complex layouts: tables, multi-column, scanned documents
- Supports multiple languages and industry-specific document types

### Deepfake Detection
Determine whether a facial image or video is authentic or AI-generated. Built on deep expertise in generative models — understanding how fakes are made is the foundation of detecting them.

- Flags manipulated media before it reaches moderation or identity checks
- Supports images, video clips, and real-time stream analysis
